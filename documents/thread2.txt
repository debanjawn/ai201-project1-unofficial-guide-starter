COMMENT by Nervous-Passion-1897:
Hello.

Not much credible information here. If you go to cunyfirst then financial and click disbursements, you can see what date your aid will be disbursed.

Also, all schools have different disbursement dates. Schools with high graduation %s like Baruch, will release aid before school starts. Schools like CSI, LAGCC, etc with high drop out rates and low graduation %, you get your aid once one or two months after the semester starts. To find the date check cunyfirst, good luck.

Also keep in mind, disbursements does not equal refund. It just means it will enter your school account
